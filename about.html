NTPC Guest Houses are accommodation facilities provided by National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC), India's largest power utility, primarily for its employees and their families. These guest houses are strategically located across various cities in India, offering a comfortable and economical stay for NTPC personnel during official and personal visits.

Tips for a Comfortable Stay

  • Early Reservations: Given the limited number of rooms, early booking is recommended.
  • Contact Management: Reach out to the guest house management for any special requirements or assistance.
  • Local Travel: Utilize local transport options like auto-rickshaws or e-rickshaws for convenient travel around the city.
